Intrinsic vs. Extrinsic Motivation

Understanding the difference between intrinsic and extrinsic motivation is crucial for personal development.

1. Intrinsic Motivation
Intrinsic motivation is driven by internal desires for learning, growth, mastery, and personal satisfaction. It is about doing something because you find it inherently rewarding and fulfilling.
2. Extrinsic Motivation
Extrinsic motivation is driven by external factors like rewards, recognition, or avoiding consequences. While it can be effective in the short term, it often lacks the lasting fulfillment that intrinsic motivation provides.
